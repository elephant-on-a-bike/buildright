How It Works

Whether you're fixing a leak, refreshing your space, or starting a small build — BuildRight makes it easy to connect with trusted local tradesmen and get competitive quotes, all while keeping your identity private.

1. Submit Your Project

Describe the work you need done — from home repairs to office renovations. Choose your region and add any helpful details. It only takes a minute.

2. Stay Anonymous

Your project is shared with vetted tradesmen in your area, but your identity stays private. No cold calls, no pressure — just quotes.

3. Compare Quotes

You’ll receive multiple offers through the platform. Compare by price, reputation, and past success — and choose the one that fits you best.

4. Pay Securely

Once you accept a quote, your payment is held in escrow. It’s only released when both you and the tradesman agree the job is complete.

5. Optional Project Management

Want a little extra help? You can opt into light-touch project management — just enough to guide things along, never enough to take over.

BuildRight gives you freedom of choice and peace of mind — connecting you with skilled professionals while protecting your time, money, and privacy.
